In the interest of informing the public of the science undertaken at the telescope, the JCMT requires a summary of the project to be provided. This summary should be succinct (one/two paragraphs) and we ask that complex concepts are simplified and acronyms expanded.

Please be aware that the summary text provided may be used at any time (once the project is accepted) and may be used before the project obtains data. For this reason please do not include any proprietary or sensitive information in this summary.

We hope the following questions may guide you in writing this summary:

  • What do you plan to observe?
  • Why is it scientifically important?
  • Why do you require the use of the JCMT?
  • Does/will your program make use of other telescopes (particularly those on Maunakea) — if so, which ones and why?
